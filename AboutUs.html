About IEEE Society

Who Are We?

We are the IEEE Student Brach at Banasthali Vidyapith. A community of passionate students, innovators, and future leaders driven by technology, learning, and growth. The IEEE Student Branch at Banasthali Vidyapith is dedicated to nurturing talent and encouraging collaboration among students from diverse disciplines who share a common passion for engineering and innovation.

Our Mission

The IEEE Society is dedicated to fostering a community where students can learn, grow, and contribute to technology and society. We host workshops, guest lectures, and networking events that enable our members to gain hands-on experience and collaborate on impactful projects.

What We Do

We organize regular events ranging from coding competitions to technical talks and panel discussions with industry experts. Our aim is to create an inclusive space for all students interested in engineering and innovation, regardless of their level of expertise.

Join Us

Whether you're passionate about technology, leadership, or event organization, there is a place for you in IEEE. Reach out via our Contact Us page to become a member or volunteer with us.
