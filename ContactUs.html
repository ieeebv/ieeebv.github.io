Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of the IEEE Student Branch?

As a student at Banasthali University, you're eligible to join the IEEE Student Branch. Visit our office during working hours or email us at ieeestudentbranch@banasthali.in for membership details and registration forms.

What benefits do I get as an IEEE Student Branch member?

Members receive access to IEEE resources, workshops, technical events, networking opportunities with industry professionals, leadership development, and recognition for technical achievements. You'll also build valuable skills through organizing and participating in branch activities.

How can I participate in IEEE events and workshops?

Event announcements are made through our social media channels, email newsletters, and notice boards. Registration instructions are provided with each announcement. IEEE members typically receive priority registration for limited-seat events.
